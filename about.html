About Us

Our Story

Your Premium Partner in Health and Safety

Located in Ottawa and proudly serving the entire province of Ontario, 2nd Nature Safety Consulting is your dedicated partner in the health and safety of your people. We believe that safety shouldn't just be a compliance checklist—it should be first nature.

Whether we are setting up training at your location, creating a custom safety management system, or supplying you with over 10,000 safety products, our goal is to integrate safety seamlessly into your daily operations.

With expertise spanning from CPO Approved Working at Heights to complex incident investigations, we bring practical, on-the-ground experience to every site we visit.
